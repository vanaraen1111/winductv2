Awards & Recognitions

ISO 9001:2008

WinDuct® complies to ISO9001:2008 Quality Management System (QMS) to ensure of the best quality and performance.

National Mark of Malaysian Brand

In year 2011, WinDuct® has been awarded with the National Mark of Malaysian Brand by SME Corporation Malaysia, a central coordinating agency to Ministry of International Trade and Industry Malaysia, to recognize the quality, excellence and distinction of WinDuct®.

IFC Certification Ltd

WinDuct® is assessed by International Fire Certification (IFC), UK which is an internationally recognized party that provides assessment to recognize the performance of high quality products.

SIRIM Quality Award 2014 - 2015

WinDuct® was the finalist in SIRIM Quality Award for product category in 2014. SIRIM Quality Award is awarded to the best of its field by SIRIM QAS International Malaysia, a recognized testing and certification agency.

WinDuct® was honoured to be the recipient of SIRIM Quality Award for product category in 2015 and is recognized as one of the best quality Malaysian product.

Responsible Care®

WinDuct® is produced with the best quality raw material, process and production to meet the standards and initiative for health, environmental and safety requirements as recognized by Reponsible Care® for sustainability.
