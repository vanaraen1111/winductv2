Quality Assurance
  1. Coating thickness can be easily controlled with simple and accurate instrument such as digital magnetic gauge after installation.
  2. Abundant quality ducting contractors and professional applicators to ensure good works.
  3. Thickness of coating will be measured on all exposed areas of the duct at 1000mm maximum intervals to ensure compliance to B.S. 8202 part 2: 1992 – Coating for fire protection of building elements. Remedial action such as recoating shall be taken if thickness deviates more than 10% from recommended value.
