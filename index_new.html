The Winduct Story

WinDuct® Fire Rated Smoke Extraction Ductwork system is a brand under Passive Fire Protection Sdn Bhd. Launched in 1996, WinDuct® has undergo extensive research and development through fire engineering excellence and quality standards to provide the best fire rated smoke extraction ductwork system. With our vision for global branding, we provide the most innovative yet practical and cost-effective solutions for your fire protection needs. At WinDuct®, we have extensive network of strategic alliances with local and foreign technology-based companies specializing in fire protection to cover your project needs and requirements and regular training is conducted to provide quality procedures to maintain the detail tested quality.

Global Distributors
WinDuct® is available through our authorized expert fire engineers and partners. Our global pressence is available through our offices in Malaysia and Vietnam and our strategic partners in Europe, Middle East, Asia, Australia and beyond.
