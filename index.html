WinDuct® Overview

WinDuct® is a fire rated smoke extraction ductwork system tested to British Standard BS476 Part 24 (Duct A and Duct B) and Australian Standard AS1530.4 (Duct A and Duct B) for 120 minutes stability, integrity and insulation.
