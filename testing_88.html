CONSTRUCTION DETAILS

2 Hours Fire Rated – Stability, Integrity and Insulation in accordance to BS 476 Pt 24:1987, ISO 6944:1985 and A.S.1530.4 for Duct B (Internal Fire)

  • G.I Duct fabricated to SMACNA or DW 144 Duct Construction Standard
  • Wincoat™ min 0.6mm thick
  • Hanger diameter sized to limit stress 10N/mm2
  • Supporting angle designed to support duct to detail
  • Wall penetration: 120kg/m3 rockwool
  • 100 x 100 x 0.8mm thick G.I angle collar coated with Wincoat™
  • 50mm thick Rockwool 120kg/m3 as Insulation of Duct for 120 minutes
  • Aluminium foil or other protective material
